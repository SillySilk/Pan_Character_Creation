## 🎭 **Character Development Examples**

### **Academic Scholar Archetype** ✅ Tested
**Background**: Well-to-Do Human from Civilized Advanced culture

**Development Path**:
1. **Childhood**: "Mentored by Elder" 
   - 📈 **Gains**: Intelligence +1, Knowledge +3, Research +2
   - 📉 **Losses**: Constitution -1, Athletics -2, Survival -1
   - 💭 **Logic**: Academic excellence through intense study creates intellectual prowess but physical weakness

2. **Apprenticeship**: "Scholar's Assistant"
   - 📈 **Gains**: Intelligence +1, Knowledge (specialized) +4
   - 📉 **Losses**: Survival -3, Social skills with common people -2
   - 💭 **Logic**: Academic specialization creates excellence but practical helplessness

**Final Result**: Brilliant scholar with encyclopedic knowledge but physically frail and socially awkward with common folk - a **realistic and interesting character**!
